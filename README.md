# Quantum Optics and Quantum Computation Research Index

This repository lists selected private research directions in quantum optics, quantum computation, continuous-variable quantum information, bosonic codes, and open quantum systems.

The working repositories are private. Public release code, technical reports, preprints, or validated examples may be linked here when ready.

## Private Research Directions

### 1. Bosonic Gaussian Simulability Atlas

Research direction: Gaussian boson sampling, hafnian and Torontonian structure, phase-space representations, classical simulability boundaries, and validation of optical sampling models.

### 2. GKP Autodiff Decoder Lab

Research direction: differentiable GKP-state modeling, bosonic decoding, error-channel optimization, continuous-variable stabilizer structure, and fault-tolerant bosonic architectures.

### 3. CV Barren Plateau Geometry

Research direction: continuous-variable variational quantum circuits, trainability geometry, symplectic parameter spaces, gradient concentration, and optimization pathologies in CV quantum machine learning.

### 4. Bosonic Error Mitigation Kernel

Research direction: bosonic noise channels, non-Gaussian resources, optical loss, mitigation kernels, and validation protocols for noisy continuous-variable quantum systems.

### 5. Dissipative Lattice GPU Lindblad

Research direction: GPU-accelerated Lindblad dynamics, driven-dissipative lattices, open quantum systems, nonequilibrium quantum optics, and scalable master-equation solvers.

## Access Policy

The working repositories are private. This public index intentionally does not expose source code, datasets, unpublished results, private notes, commit history, or repository links.
